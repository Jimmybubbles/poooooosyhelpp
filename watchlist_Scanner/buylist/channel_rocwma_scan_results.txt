LEGEND:
  Zone: Where price is in the channel
    - Bottom Third (0-33%): BEST buy zone at support
    - Lower Half (33-50%): GOOD buy zone
    - Middle (50-66%): FAIR, wait for pullback

  Signal Date: When ROCWMA triangle UP occurred
    - Shows the exact date momentum shifted bullish
    - 'Days' column shows days since the signal

  ROCWMA: ROC-Weighted MA oscillator state
    - bull: Bullish momentum
    - Triangle UP: Reversal signal from bearish to bullish

  R:R: Risk/Reward ratio
    - Risk: Distance to channel low (stop loss)
    - Reward: Distance to channel high (target)

  Profit%: Potential upside to channel high

TRADING STRATEGY:

  ENTRY TRIGGERS:
    1. Price in lower half of channel (preferably bottom third)
    2. ROCWMA triangle UP appears OR already bull state
    3. Stock in uptrend (pullback buy, not falling knife)

  ENTRY: Buy when ROCWMA shows triangle UP inside channel
         Enter BEFORE breakout arrow for better risk/reward

  STOP LOSS: Below channel low (or recent swing low)

  TARGET:
    - Conservative: Channel high (resistance)
    - Aggressive: Channel breakout + channel range
